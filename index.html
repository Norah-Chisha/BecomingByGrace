Introduction

Hello beautiful souls, and welcome to Becoming By Grace — a space where faith, purpose, growth, and my journey in tech come together.

My name is Norah Chisha, a Computer Science student, an aspiring Software Engineer, motivational writer, and above all, a woman growing daily in God’s grace.

I created this blog because I believe each of us is constantly becoming — learning, unlearning, healing, building, failing forward, and allowing God to shape us into who He has called us to be.

Here, you will find a blend of:

  • Faith & Identity — discovering who we are in Christ
  • Tech Journey — my experiences as a CS student
  • Mindset & Motivation — quotes, reflections, and life lessons
  • Stories — creative writing and short stories with meaning

My prayer is simple: That something here speaks to your heart. That you leave each post feeling encouraged, strengthened, and reminded that you are becoming — daily, slowly, beautifully — by the grace of God. Welcome to Becoming by Grace. Let’s grow together.

About Me

Hi, I’m Norah Chisha, a passionate Computer Science student and aspiring Software Engineer. Beyond learning code and algorithms, I am a motivational writer, and a lover of God who believes in living with purpose and intention.

I started this blog to share my journey — spiritually, mentally, and academically. My mission is simple: to inspire, uplift, and encourage others to become who God has called them to be. Through faith, mindset growth, and personal development, I hope to be a light to someone navigating their own path.

Whether it’s tech lessons, spiritual reflections, creative writing, or motivation, everything I share comes from a place of authenticity and growth. I’m still becoming — and I believe you are too.

Welcome once again. Let’s grow together.
